At first glance at the top image, it is obvious that this cake is homemade. But the hidden message of this image is that it was my first time baking a cake for my father's birthday. It was during the period of Covid so we didn't want to take the risk of going out to buy a cake so I made this matcha cake with fruit on top to celebrate his birthday. My original theme is on a car show but now I change it to the theme of "First time". I will be gathering pictures that tell my first time. This image relates to my topic because it was my first time baking a cake. I might use this image as one of my collections too.
